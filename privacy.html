Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

At AI Resume Pro, we take your privacy seriously. Here's what you need to know:

🔒 No Data Storage

We do NOT store any of your personal information on our servers. All your resume data stays in your browser's local storage and is never sent to our servers.

🤖 API Usage

When you generate a resume, your information is sent directly to the AI service (Claude or Gemini) for processing. This is done through secure HTTPS connections, and we don't intercept or store this data.

💾 Local Storage

Your resume data is saved in your browser's local storage for convenience. You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser data.

🍪 No Cookies

We don't use tracking cookies or analytics on this site.

📧 Contact

If you have any privacy concerns, please contact us at privacy@airesumepro.com
